U.S. employers add 209,000 jobs in July

By Associated Press on Aug 4th 2017 with 44 comments

U.S. employers added 209,000 jobs in July, a second straight month of robust gains that underscore the economy's vitality as it enters a ninth year of expansion.

The Labor Department said Friday that the unemployment rate slipped to 4.3%, matching a 16-year low first reached in May.

Growth in Americans' paychecks, however, remains stubbornly slow. Average hourly pay rose by 2.5% from a year earlier, the same tepid annual pace as in June.
